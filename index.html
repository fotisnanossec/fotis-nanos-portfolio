About Me

A unique career journey from artistic discipline to digital defense.

Bridging Two Worlds

My path is a testament to the power of transferable skills. With a diploma in Electronics, Computer, and Network Technology from Technical Vocational School (TEE), Athens (1998–2002), and years as a professional concert guitarist, I honed meticulous attention to detail, rigorous practice, and understanding of complex structures. These principles form the bedrock of my transition to cybersecurity.

In music, I analyze scores for patterns and challenges. In cybersecurity, I apply that same analytical rigor to dissect network traffic, identify vulnerabilities, and defend against threats. The creative problem-solving for arranging music or overcoming passages mirrors developing security solutions.

This unique fusion of an artistic past, an early technical foundation, and self-driven cybersecurity training equips me with a rare combination of technical proficiency and strategic foresight. I am a dedicated analyst and artist, bringing a disciplined, creative, and proactive approach to every challenge.
